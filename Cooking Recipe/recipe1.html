Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  4. Add the tomato puree, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, cumin, and salt. Stir to combine.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
