Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 300g spinach, blanched and pureed
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cream for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add the tomato puree, garam masala, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add the spinach puree and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the paneer cubes and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh cream and serve hot with roti or naan.
