Chole

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas until they are soft. Set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add the onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add the tomato puree, coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  6. Add the cooked chickpeas and mix well. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or bhature.
