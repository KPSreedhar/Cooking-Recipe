Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Oil for frying
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • Rose water (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the milk powder, all-purpose flour, ghee, milk, and baking soda to form a smooth dough. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare the sugar syrup by heating the sugar, water, and cardamoms in a pot. Boil until the syrup thickens slightly. Add rose water if using.
  3. Form small balls from the dough, making sure there are no cracks.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the balls until golden brown, turning occasionally.
  5. Soak the fried balls in the warm sugar syrup for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  6. Serve warm or at room temperature.
